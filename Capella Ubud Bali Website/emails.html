Communication Standards

Email Standards

1. Email Proposal

Email proposal digunakan untuk mengirimkan penawaran / proposal reservasi kepada calon tamu sebelum booking dikonfirmasi. Berisi detail kamar, tarif, fasilitas, dan informasi penting lainnya.

Contoh Email Proposal:

Subject: Reservation Proposal - [Guest Name] - [Property Name]

Dear Mr./Ms. [Guest Last Name],

Warmest greetings from [Property Name].

As per our conversation, we are pleased to propose the following reservation details for your upcoming stay:

Guest Name: [Full Name]
Arrival Date: [Date]
Departure Date: [Date]
Number of Nights: [X] nights
Room Type: [Room Category]
Rate: [Currency] [Amount] ++ per night
Inclusions: Breakfast, Wi-Fi, etc.
Cancellation Policy: Free cancellation until [Date]

Please let us know if you wish to proceed with this booking. We look forward to welcoming you to [Property Name].

Should you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
Reservations Department
[Property Name]
2. Confirmation Letter

Confirmation letter dikirim setelah booking dikonfirmasi. Berisi detail reservasi yang sudah pasti, nomor konfirmasi, dan informasi penting lainnya.

Contoh Confirmation Letter:

Subject: Reservation Confirmation - [Guest Name] - [Confirmation Number]

Dear Mr./Ms. [Guest Last Name],

Warmest greetings from [Property Name].

Thank you for choosing [Property Name]. We are pleased to confirm your reservation as follows:

Confirmation Number: [XXXXXX]
Guest Name: [Full Name]
Arrival Date: [Date]
Departure Date: [Date]
Number of Nights: [X] nights
Room Type: [Room Category]
Rate: [Currency] [Amount] ++ per night
Total Amount: [Currency] [Total] (incl. tax & service)
Inclusions: Breakfast, Wi-Fi, etc.
Cancellation Policy: Free cancellation until [Date]
Payment Method: [Credit Card / Bank Transfer / On Arrival]

Please present this confirmation letter upon check-in.

We look forward to welcoming you to [Property Name]. Should you need any assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
Reservations Department
[Property Name]
3. Reconfirmation

Reconfirmation dikirim untuk mengkonfirmasi ulang reservasi atau meminta konfirmasi dari tamu sebelum kedatangan.

Contoh Reconfirmation Email:

Subject: Reservation Reconfirmation - [Guest Name] - [Property Name]

Dear Mr./Ms. [Guest Last Name],

Warmest greetings from [Property Name].

We would like to reconfirm your upcoming reservation with us:

Confirmation Number: [XXXXXX]
Arrival Date: [Date]
Departure Date: [Date]
Room Type: [Room Category]

Kindly confirm if there are any changes to your travel plans. Please let us know your estimated time of arrival so we can prepare for your arrival.

Should you wish to add any special requests or preferences, please feel free to let us know.

We look forward to welcoming you soon.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
Reservations Department
[Property Name]
4. Cancellation

Cancellation email dikirim untuk mengkonfirmasi pembatalan reservasi sesuai permintaan tamu.

Contoh Cancellation Email:

Subject: Reservation Cancellation Confirmation - [Guest Name] - [Confirmation Number]

Dear Mr./Ms. [Guest Last Name],

Warmest greetings from [Property Name].

We acknowledge your request to cancel the following reservation:

Confirmation Number: [XXXXXX]
Original Arrival Date: [Date]
Original Departure Date: [Date]
Room Type: [Room Category]

We hereby confirm that the above reservation has been cancelled as per your request.

[If within free cancellation period:] No cancellation fee will be charged.
[If outside free cancellation period:] A cancellation fee of [amount] will be charged as per the cancellation policy.

We hope to have the opportunity to welcome you at [Property Name] in the future.

Should you need any further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
Reservations Department
[Property Name]
