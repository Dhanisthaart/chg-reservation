OPERA VIP CODE

All Capella Properties embrace a standardized VIP code classification, ensuring seamless alignment of VIP classifications and accurate handling of preferences and details across the entire property portfolio. This unified approach to VIP codes is pivotal for the successful onboarding of Opera Cloud, allowing all properties to optimize the utilization of shared profiles and preferences for maximum benefit and guest recognition.
