Cloud-Based Property Management

OPERA Cloud

1. Room Availability (Room Diary)

  • Go to Booking > Reservations > Room Diary
  • Input the Date when you want to start your search from. And select any other relevant detail for your search such as "Room", "Room Type", etc. And then click Search
  • Results will be displayed at the bottom of the screen.

2. How to create a booking

  • Go to Booking > Reservations > Look To Book Sales Screen
  • Enter the caller/contact information and then click New Reservation
  • Fill in the stay details:
    - Arrival date
    - Number of nights
    - Rooms
    - Adults / Children
  • Click Search to display room type & rate availability

3. How to cancel a reservation

  • Go to Booking > Reservations > Manage Reservations
  • Search the name of the Reservation with any relevant information (Guest name, Reservation Number or etc.)
  • Click on the Confirmation Number to open the guest reservation
  • When the reservation of the guest is open on the screen, find the I Want To… button on the right-hand side of the screen and click on it.
Step 1: Click I Want To button
Step 2: Choose Cancel Reservation
Step 3: Select cancellation reason
Step 4: Confirm cancellation
Step 5: Cancellation processed
Step 6: Cancellation confirmation
Step 7: Reservation cancelled
